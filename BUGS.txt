Known bugs found in this distribution, which are normally not in GnuCOBOL 1.1:

* gettext is a little bit borked, some command line options are not recognized correctly,
  at least -c (Compile and assemble, but do not link) which is misinterpreted
  as -conf (compiler configuration file)
* intrinsic FUNCTIONs returning always zero:
  ANNUITY, EXP, LOG, LOG10, SQRT, STANDARD-DEVIATION, TAN
